Welcome to Korea's Royal Heritage

For more than 600 years, Korea's royal palaces have represented the country's history, culture, and artistic traditions. This website introduces the most famous palaces and explains why they remain important cultural landmarks today.

Featured Palace: Gyeongbokgung

Gyeongbokgung is the largest and most famous royal palace in Seoul. Built in 1395, it served as the main palace of the Joseon Dynasty and is known for its beautiful architecture, grand gates, and scenic mountain backdrop.
